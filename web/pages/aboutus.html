About Us

History

Welcome to the Jockey’s Convention Center (JCC), a renowned conference center located in the heart of Jakarta, Indonesia. Established in 1974, JCC has been at the forefront of hosting important national and international events, making it one of the earliest and most popular convention centers in the city.

Over the years, JCC has played a pivotal role in facilitating a wide range of conferences, exhibitions, fairs, indoor sports events, and musical concerts. As a premier venue, we have proudly hosted distinguished gatherings, including the 11th Non-Aligned Movement Conference and the historic Asian-African Conference.

Our commitment to excellence and our state-of-the-art facilities have made JCC the preferred choice for numerous organizations, both local and international, seeking an exceptional venue for their events. Our professional and experienced team is dedicated to ensuring the success of every occasion held at JCC, providing unparalleled service and support from start to finish.

Our client portfolio includes presidents, CEOs, and International musicians, as well as companies from all spheres and levels of business. Our engagements portfolio includes world conventions, regional conferences, private business meetings, theatrical performances, music festivals, concerts, fashion shows, sport events, weddings, dinners, and private launches. Whether you are directing a ballet, participating in an extreme adventure exhibition, or launching the latest line of environmentally-friendly products, the Jockey’s Conference Center is here to showcase all aspects of your expertise and brand-and under just one roof.
